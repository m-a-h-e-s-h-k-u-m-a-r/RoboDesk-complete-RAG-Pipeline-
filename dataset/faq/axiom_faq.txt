AXIOM ROBOTICS — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

=== GENERAL QUESTIONS ===

Q: What is a collaborative robot (cobot)?
A: A collaborative robot, or cobot, is a robot designed to work safely alongside humans without requiring safety fencing in most applications. Axiom cobots (AX-100 and AX-200) use force/torque sensing and collision detection to stop automatically when they contact a person. This makes them ideal for small spaces and tasks requiring human-robot teamwork.

Q: Do I need programming experience to use an Axiom robot?
A: No. The Axiom Studio software provides a drag-and-drop interface that allows users with no programming background to create robot programs. For advanced users, Python SDK and ROS 2 integration are available. Most customers are up and running within one day of installation.

Q: What industries use Axiom robots?
A: Axiom robots are used across manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, food and beverage, electronics, automotive, aerospace, and education. The AX-100 is popular in labs and small workshops; the AX-200 in mid-size manufacturing; the AX-500 in heavy industry; and the AX-Mobile in warehouses and hospitals.

Q: Can Axiom robots work 24/7?
A: Yes. All Axiom robot arms (AX-100, AX-200, AX-500) are designed for continuous 24/7 operation. The AX-Mobile has a 10-hour battery life and requires a 2-hour recharge cycle. Planned maintenance every 6 months is recommended for optimal performance.

=== TECHNICAL QUESTIONS ===

Q: What is the payload capacity of each model?
A: AX-100: 3 kg, AX-200: 5 kg, AX-500: 20 kg, AX-Mobile: 150 kg (transport payload).

Q: What is the reach of each robot arm model?
A: AX-100: 600 mm, AX-200: 850 mm, AX-500: 1,300 mm.

Q: What programming languages are supported?
A: All models support Axiom Studio (visual programming), Python SDK, and ROS 2. The AX-200 and AX-500 additionally support REST API integration. The AX-500 also supports OPC-UA for industrial system integration.

Q: Is safety fencing required?
A: For the AX-100 and AX-200 cobots, safety fencing is typically not required when operating within collaborative speed limits. A risk assessment is always recommended. The AX-500 industrial robot requires safety fencing due to its high speed and payload.

Q: What is the IP rating of each model?
A: AX-100: IP54, AX-200: IP54, AX-500: IP65, AX-Mobile: IP52. IP65 means the AX-500 is fully dust-tight and protected against water jets.

Q: Can I integrate Axiom robots with my existing ERP or MES system?
A: Yes. The AX-200 and AX-500 support REST API integration, allowing connection to most ERP and MES systems. The AX-500 also supports OPC-UA, which is the standard protocol for industrial automation systems.

Q: How accurate are Axiom robots?
A: Repeatability specifications: AX-100: ±0.05 mm, AX-200: ±0.03 mm, AX-500: ±0.02 mm. These figures represent the robot's ability to return to the same position repeatedly, which is critical for precision assembly tasks.

=== SUPPORT AND MAINTENANCE ===

Q: What is included in the standard warranty?
A: All Axiom robots include a 1-year parts and labor warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, unauthorized modifications, or normal wear items such as cables and end-effectors.

Q: How do I get technical support?
A: Technical support is available via phone (+1-800-AXIOM-01), email (support@axiomrobotics.com), and live chat on our website. Support hours are Monday–Friday, 8 AM–8 PM EST. Emergency support for production-critical issues is available 24/7 for customers with a Premium Support contract.

Q: How often does the robot need maintenance?
A: Axiom recommends preventive maintenance every 6 months or 4,000 operating hours, whichever comes first. Maintenance includes joint lubrication, cable inspection, software updates, and safety system verification.

Q: Can I perform maintenance myself?
A: Basic maintenance tasks (software updates, cable inspection) can be performed by trained operators. Joint lubrication and mechanical inspections should be performed by Axiom-certified technicians. Unauthorized disassembly voids the warranty.
