AXIOM ROBOTICS — WARRANTY AND SUPPORT POLICY

=== STANDARD WARRANTY ===

Coverage Period
All Axiom Robotics products are covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of delivery to the original purchaser.

What Is Covered
The standard warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
- Defective electronic components (controller boards, sensors, motors)
- Software defects that prevent normal operation
- Structural defects in the robot arm and base

What Is NOT Covered
The standard warranty does not cover:
- Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or negligence
- Damage caused by unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Normal wear items: cables, connectors, end-effectors, grippers, and seals
- Damage caused by operating outside specified environmental conditions
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents) that does not affect functionality
- Damage caused by power surges or improper electrical installation
- Consumable items: lubricants, filters, and batteries (AX-Mobile)

Warranty Claim Process
1. Contact Axiom support at support@axiomrobotics.com or +1-800-AXIOM-01
2. Provide proof of purchase, robot serial number, and description of the defect
3. Axiom will issue a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 2 business days
4. Ship the defective unit or component to the designated Axiom service center
5. Axiom will repair or replace the defective item within 10 business days of receipt
6. Repaired or replaced items are covered for the remainder of the original warranty period or 90 days, whichever is longer

=== SPARE PARTS AND REPAIRS ===

Spare Parts Availability
Axiom guarantees spare parts availability for a minimum of 7 years after the product's end-of-sale date. Critical spare parts are stocked at regional service centers for next-day delivery.

Out-of-Warranty Repairs
Repairs outside the warranty period are billed at:
- Labor: $150/hour
- Parts: List price
- Minimum diagnostic fee: $250 (applied toward repair cost if repair is approved)

Repair Turnaround Time
- Standard repair: 10–15 business days
- Expedited repair: 5–7 business days (50% surcharge)
- Emergency repair: 2–3 business days (100% surcharge)
